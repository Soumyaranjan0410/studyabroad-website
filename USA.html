WHY STUDY IN USA

The moment you decide to study abroad, there are many things to take into consideration. It is a matter for your future; therefore, it is essential to introspect before taking the decision. However, one such country that you can trust for your future is USA. According to a report, the USA has emerged as a leading country known for its quality education. Deciding to study in USA might be one of your best decisions. The reasons why students choose to study in the USA:

  1. COURSE CURRICULUM: USA has amalgamated the needs of industry in its education system. Very often Universities have industry leaders as their board members or advisory counsel. This is to ensure that the University curriculum is in sync with the real time requirements. The curriculum is constantly updated therefore resulting in gradates who are ready to join the work force.
  2. RESEARCH FACILITIES & TECHNOLOGY: 80% of Nobel laureate are part of USA educational system in different roles. This imparts a unique opportunity for every student to be part of research projects and get first-hand experience of cutting edge technology. Deserving students are made part of ongoing research which not only gives them confidence but also prepare them for challenges ahead.
  3. FLEXIBILITY: Students are free to select courses across different streams as well as design the course curriculum as per their pace of study. Also, USA offers programs across all streams. The course curriculum has been designed to ensure that the students develop into well rounded individuals rather than just grasping the subject knowledge.
  4. SAFETY & FACILITIES FOR INTERNATIONAL STUDENTS: Its among the safest destination and provides all the amenities required for a new student coming from abroad. Most universities have specialized security for campus who secure the entry and exit points and man the campus area regularly.
  5. WORK PERMITS & JOB OPPORTUNITIES: Optional Practical Training (OPT) is provided for all students which give them an opportunity to practice their skills in USA itself, which often results in them getting Work visas [H1]. The students are also eligible for Curriculum Practical Training [CPT] after studying for 2 semesters i.e. Full-time internships in the students area of expertise.
  6. INTERNATIONAL EXPOSURE: USA attracts the brightest and best students from almost all countries of the globe. This gives the student international exposure and makes them a global citizen.
  7. RECOGNITION OF DEGREE: The degree from USA is recognized world over and is given lot of weight while applying for Job or Higher Studies.

The USA offers numerous options to choose from according to your educational and cultural preferences. Moreover, many USA universities and colleges offer the best facilities for many international students.

Education By The Best Professor

Top students of the world should try for admission into top universities of the USA. The best professors using the latest techniques and latest curriculum conduct these classes. Moreover, depending on your course, the professors conduct a real-life situation that you have to solve using your knowledge and intelligence. This way, the teachers prepare you to face the real world.

Course Curriculum

The majority of students prefer to study masters in USA as it offers multiple courses. Some of the masterclasses are given by the industry leaders to give clear guidance to the students. This is to ensure that the University curriculum is coordinated with real-time requirements.

How Much Will It Cost To Study In USA?

Comparing with other international countries, you may find it surprising that the USA is quite affordable. Moreover, international students get an opportunity to work while studying. This is the first road toward financial independence for such students. According to some reports, it is seen that study in USA for Indian students is quite affordable than for others. Therefore, every Indian student who is thinking to go abroad for higher studies should consider the USA.

Education System In USA

The education system in the USA has considered one of the best education systems compared to others. This is also a reason why many students prefer to study in USA universities and colleges.

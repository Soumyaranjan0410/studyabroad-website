WHY STUDY IN FRANCE?

France has always been at the forefront of human Civilization from the earliest times through middle ages. It has always been a hub for cultural and industrial innovations. The contribution of French revolution to usher in an era of nation states and democracy can never be undermined. The renaissance era had seen an unparalleled growth in Arts and culture which has continued till today.

Despite a downturn during second world war wherein France took the brunt of Nazi expansionism, France recovered to attain the former glory. France today is considered among the most industrialized nations with unique culture and is a welfare state for its citizen.

France has always been home to worldâ€™s top most universities and that tradition has continued till date . France has 83 public funded universities apart from a host of private universities and specialized institutes. There are more than 3.10 lakhs, international students, studying in France at present.Below are some reasons why students chose France are:

  • Tradition of Excellence & Top ranked Universities
  • Cultural Hub & Open Society
  • Programs in English
  • Opportunity to learn French
  • Internship is an integral part of Course Curriculum
  • Post Study Work Permit

France has one of the most respected scholastic systems in the world. Thousands of international students choose France as their educational destination for various reasons. Whether you are an individual pursuing undergraduate studies, postgraduate, or doctorate, an amazing education can easily be found in the country.

For deserving and worthy students, many universities offer various types of scholarship who plan to study in France.

Some Of The Benefits To The Indian Students When They Decide To Study In France:

  • Easy visa process
  • Slighter chances of visa cancellation
  • Job placement back in India as well
  • Without IELTS
  • Low fee options available
  • Quality Education
  • Paid internship
To get a chance to study in France for Indian students is an excellent opportunity for every Indian student.

How Much Will It Cost To Study In France?

There are almost 83 Universities in France, which are supported by the national government. The government of France provides education at a reasonable cost to understudies. Comparing with other international countries, you may find it shocking that France is quite reasonable. Moreover, international students get an opportunity to work part-time while studying. The study in France cost is very much considerable than the US, UK, and Australia.

The Education System In France

The education system used in France universities is considered one of the best education systems compared to other foreign institutes. This is also a reason why many students prefer to study in France universities and colleges.

The professors in France universities use the latest techniques and latest curriculum to conduct classes. Depending on your area of the field, the professors conduct a situation that you have to solve using your skill and intelligence. This is one of the ways; teachers prepare you to face the real world.

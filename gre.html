GRE â€“ General Overview


Graduate Record Examinations, also know as (GRE) is a regulated test to meet the admissions requirement for most Graduate Schools in the United States. It's owned & administered by Educational Testing Service (ETS) It aims to measure skills that have been acquired over a long period of learning like Verbal Reasoning, Quantitative Reasoning, Analytical Writing, & Critical Thinking. The GRE score can range from being a mere admission criterion to a critical selection factor..

Exam Pattern

ANALYTICAL WRITING

QUESTIONS : Two Essays
TIME: 30 minute each
QUESTIONS TYPES: Analysis of an issue, Analysis of an Argument

QUANTITATIVE SECTIONS [2]

QUESTIONS : Approx.20 per section
TIME: 35 minutes per section
QUESTIONS TYPES: Quantitative Comparison, Problem Solving, Data Interpretation

VERBAL SECTIONS [2]

QUESTIONS : Approx.20 per section
TIME: 30 minutes per section
QUESTIONS TYPES: Text Completion, Sentence Equivalence, Reading Comprehension

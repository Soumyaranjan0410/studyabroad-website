About Us

Home / About Us

Intro

Quite aptly is quoted by Benjamin Franklin, “An investment in knowledge pays the best interest”. At SAAC Education Abroad, we believe in this same philosophy and therefore always endeavor to provide the highest quality service and support to fulfilling your dream of studying overseas. Our highly experienced team of counselors headed by our Director Ms Aboli Mirje provide you with complete support in achieving your study abroad dream right from the moment the idea comes to your mind, until you achieve your desired qualification in your choice of location and University.

How It All Started

What started as a simple idea in the town of Sangli, Maharashtra in the year 2016, has transformed into a reality by our Director Ms Aboli Mirje, who has been spearheading the process of building relations with the top Universities across UK, Ireland, Canada, USA and many other countries where our students are now successfully placed for Bachelor and Master’s level programmes in Information Technology, Business, Engineering, Accounting, Finance, Law, Genetics, Genomic Counselling, Physics, Fashion, Brandingand, and many more. Our office in Pune was established in 2017 which doesn’t seem like a long time ago, however, the number of successfully placed candidates speak for themselves regarding the success SAAC Education Abroad has gained within this short period.

Ms Mirje has very carefully picked her team of counselors and advisors who share the same vision as herself of assisting students in achieving their academic goals. These individuals have vast firsthand experience and extensive knowledge of procedures and trends of overseas education and life in a foreign nation. Therefore, they are more than willing to guide you accurately towards the same. Walk into our centrally located office in Pune as well as Sangli and we promise that you would certainly not be disappointed about making the choice of associating with us.

  • UPFRONT SERVICES: We make sure to take special care and custom modify our services so that they are completely transparent and happen in constant conjunction with student’s participation.

  • FRIENDLY COUNSELORS: Our team of counselors are the best you could get. Don’t take our word for it. One counselling session with them and we guarantee that you would have no more confusion about any details or information relevant to any course, University or country.
  • INHOUSE IELTS/ PTE COACHING: We have our highly experienced British Council and IDP certified IELTS and Pearson certified PTE trainer who has trained students across the globe and drawn them one step closer towards their dream.

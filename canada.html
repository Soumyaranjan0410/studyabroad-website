WHY STUDY IN CANADA?

Canada has been voted the best place to live & the best place to study, while also ranking high for working, visiting and investing. Canada is not only famous for its beautiful scenery or food; it is also famous for its amazing academic infrastructure. Canada is one of the top international student destinations as almost 353,000 students come to Canada for educational purposes.

These are the reasons why many students preferred to study in Canada

  1. INTERNATIONALLY RECOGNIZED DEGREE: Canadian degree, diploma or certificate is accepted globally owing to its quality education and competitive entry criteria. Canadian graduates are highly sought after by employers due to their high standards of academic excellence.
  2. AFFORDABLE EDUCATION: The quality of education and living standards in Canada are amongst the highest in the world. The Cost of Living and Tuition Fees are much lesser compared to the USA and UK.
  3. EDUCATION SYSTEM: Canada is one of the top international education performers, hosting an array of quality educational institutions offering technical and professional degrees, diplomas and certificate courses in various domains.
  4. LIBERAL IMMIGRATION: International students with Canadian credentials and work experience may apply for citizenship in Canada without leaving Canada.
  5. POSSIBILITY TO WORK: International students may work on-campus without a work permit. Study permit holders in Canada may gain work experience by working off-campus while their studies. As of June 1, 2014, a student may qualify to work off-campus up to20 hours per week without a work permit.
  6. MULTICULTURAL SOCIETY AND A PLEASANT DESTINATION: Canada is a democratic country. It encourages multicultural society through the Canadian constitution and the Canadian charter of rights and freedom, the two government bodies strive to preserve the rights of the people living in the country. All are equal and everyone has the right to be social, culturally and economically active. People there, are readily accepting and respectful of the differences.

How Much Will It Cost To Study In Canada?

A majority of student believes that studying abroad will cost a fortune; however, studying in Canada is affordable. Even though Canadian international student has to pay higher tuition fees than domestic students, however, it is still cheaper than the educational cost of the United Nations, the United Kingdom, and Australia.

Many Canadian universities provide hostel facilities; however, for any reason, you do not want to stay in a hostel you can easily arrange a house for rent with or without roommates.

The moment you combine average tuition fees and the cost of living, you’ll see that Canada is clearly the most reasonably priced option for overseas study. Talk with a consultant and get full information on study in Canada’s cost price according to your country’s money value.

The Education System Of Canada

An educational degree from a Canadian university is widely recognized as well as equal to one from the US, UK, or Australia. Canadian universities are steadily performing well in international educational rankings. According to a study in 2016, three Canadian universities were ranked in the top 50 universities in the world.

Not only universities Canadian institutes, schools, vocational schools, and colleges also hold the same reputation. Moreover, the study material of the Canadian Universities is truly remarkable as well as the teachers take regular tests and assignments to keep the students on track. There are many masters programs in Canada for both national and international students.
